Goal of the study

Detect a significant discrepancy between the prevalence of comedies in the official competition and their success rate across the three primary award categories.

Data Collection

We built a database of all films selected for the Official Competition at Cannes by scraping the relevant Wikipedia pages. We then enriched this dataset with additional information about each film, including genre tags, the director’s name, the year of selection, and whether the film was merely selected for the Official Competition or went on to win the Palme d’Or, the Grand Prix, or the Jury Prize. In total, we collected data on N = 1,802 films spanning the period from 1946 to 2025.

Comedy Classification

For this analysis, we chose to classify comedy based on the wikipedia genre tags.


The level comedy is 0 (No Comedy) if the Comedy tag is absent from the list of genres.

The level comedy is 1 (Mixed Comedy) if the Comedy tag is present alongside at least one of the following genres: Drama, Action, Romance, or Horror.

The level comedy is 2 (Pure Comedy) if the Comedy tag is present, and none of the four genres above appear.

Results

The mean comedy level of films among Palme d'Or winners is slightly lower than among nominated films (μw = 0.218, μn = 0.242), but this difference is not statistically significant (t = 0.523, p = 0.602). Similarly, the mean comedy level of films among Grand Prix winners is also slightly lower than among nominated films (μw = 0.227, μn = 0.242), and this difference is not statistically significant either (t = 0.233, p = 0.816). Finally, the mean comedy level of films among Jury Prize winners is slightly higher than among nominated films (μw = 0.259, μn = 0.249), but this difference is not statistically significant (t = -0.175, p = 0.862).


Overall, none of these comparisons reveal a meaningful difference in comedy level. However, it is worth noting that a large number of films in the Official Selection are classified as non-comedy, which suggests that comedy remains relatively uncommon in the competition lineup.
